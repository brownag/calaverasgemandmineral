# Club History

The Calaveras Gem and Mineral Society was established in 1948 and continues to flourish with a membership dedicated to sharing knowledge and interests in Rocks and Minerals, both among members and with the public.

The Society is a member of the California Federation of Mineralogical Societies (CFMS) and the American Federation of Mineralogical Societies (AFMS).

## Our Mission

To promote education, scientific advancement, and public awareness of mineralogy, geology, and lapidary arts through meetings, field trips, exhibitions, and public outreach.

## Clubhouse

![Clubhouse Overview](../images/ch_blr.jpg)

Our clubhouse is located nestled among the oak trees in the Mother Lode foothills near Angels Camp, California. It serves as our meeting space and home to our lapidary shop.
