## Equipment Available

- Rock and trim saws
- Cabbing units
- Polishers

## Shop Rules

- Members may provide instruction to other members
- Work on your own projects during shop hours
- Follow all safety rules and equipment procedures
- Help maintain the shop for future use

Regular shop access is available to current club members. For membership information, see [Membership Information](membership.html).

## Special Hours

The shop may have extended hours during special events or tailgate sales. Check with the club for current information.
