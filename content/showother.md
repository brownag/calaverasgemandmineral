# Show Activities & More

## Activities for Children

Fun and interesting activities for children including games, demonstrations, and hands-on experiences with rocks and minerals.

## Door Prizes

Throughout the show, we draw door prizes from our visitors. You might win a great gem or mineral specimen!

## Raffle

Our raffle features a variety of rocks and minerals. Purchase raffle tickets for a chance to win amazing prizes.

## Silent Auction

Our silent auction will have a wide variety of rocks and minerals. Place your bid on items of interest throughout the show.

## Food Booth

Enjoy delicious food and refreshments at our food booth. Hungry shoppers can grab snacks and meals while browsing the show.

## Parking & Shuttle Service

**FREE PARKING** is available at the Calaveras County Fairgrounds.

**Free shuttle service** is provided from the parking lot to Mark Twain Hall where the show is held.
