# Membership

## Benefits

Membership in the Calaveras Gem and Mineral Society offers many benefits including:

- Access to monthly meetings and events
- Use of the lapidary shop
- Participation in field trips
- Access to the members area
- Connection with fellow rock and mineral enthusiasts
