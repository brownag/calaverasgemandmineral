## Directions

The fairgrounds are located in Calaveras County near Angels Camp, off Highway 49.

### Using GPS or Maps

Use the address above with Google Maps, Apple Maps, or other navigation apps:

**2456 Gunclub Road, Angels Camp, CA 95222**

## Parking

**FREE PARKING** is available at the fairgrounds.

**Free shuttle service** is provided from the parking lot to Mark Twain Hall where our show is held.
