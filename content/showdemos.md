# Show Demonstrations

Talented demonstrators will show how lapidary and jewelry items are made.

## Lapidary Demonstrations

Learn from talented artisans how raw rocks are transformed into beautiful finished pieces through:

- Cutting and shaping techniques
- Polishing methods
- Finishing processes
- Equipment operation

## Jewelry Making Demonstrations

See skilled craftspeople demonstrate:

- Jewelry design and creation
- Metalworking techniques
- Stone setting methods
- Finishing and polishing

## Schedule

Demonstrations run throughout the show on both Saturday and Sunday.
