# Show Exhibits

Outstanding case displays will feature fine mineral, lapidary and jewelry by members and guest exhibitors.

## Featured Exhibits

Our show showcases beautiful displays including:

- Fine mineral specimens
- Lapidary artwork
- Jewelry creations
- Cut and polished stones
- Educational displays
- Fluorescent mineral displays
- Fossil displays

## Guest Exhibitors

We welcome guest exhibitors to display their finest work. Exhibits are a great way to share knowledge and appreciation for rocks, minerals, and lapidary arts.
