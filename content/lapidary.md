# Lapidary Shop

The Calaveras Gem and Mineral Society maintains a well-equipped lapidary shop for its members.

## Equipment

![Lapidary Shop Interior](../images/lap_indx.jpg)

Our shop includes:

- Rock and trim saws
- Cabbing units
- Polishers
- Other lapidary equipment

## Shop Hours

Regular shop hours are **Saturdays, 10AM to 2PM**.

During this time, club members can provide instruction for other members or work on their own projects.
