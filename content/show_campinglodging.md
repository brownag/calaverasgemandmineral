# Show Camping & Lodging

## Accommodations in Angels Camp

The Calaveras County area has many lodging options for visitors attending our show.

### Hotels & Motels

Various hotels and motels are available in the Angels Camp area and surrounding communities. The [Calaveras Visitors Bureau](https://www.gocalaveras.com) can provide current information on accommodations.

### Bed & Breakfasts

The Mother Lode area is home to many charming bed and breakfast establishments.

### Camping

Several campgrounds are available in the area:

- RV parks and camping facilities
- National Forest camping areas
- Local parks with camping amenities
