# Field Trips

The club is working on dates for future field trips. In addition to sponsoring its own field trips, our members are welcome to attend trips hosted by other Field Trip Chairmen's Association ("Co-op") clubs and the California Federation of Mineralogical Societies (CFMS).

## Affiliated Organizations

- [Field Trip Chairmen's Association](https://www.ourfieldtrips.org)
- [California Federation of Mineralogical Societies (CFMS)](https://www.cfmsinc.org)
- [American Federation of Mineralogical Societies (AFMS)](https://www.amfed.org)

For more information about field trips, contact us at calaverasgemandmineralsociety@gmail.com.
