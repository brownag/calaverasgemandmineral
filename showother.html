OTHER SHOW EVENTS
 

We will have enjoyable events throughout the show.
  • Children's Activities -
    • Children can participate in a free treasureless treasure hunt where they look for various items in the exhibit cases.
    • They can search for red garnets in a sample of sand, decorate a pet rock with colorful felt, feathers, pipe cleansers, eyes, and cotton balls, or assemble a colorful bracelet from plastic beads and flexible string.
    • There will be a spinning wheel where every spin will result in a winner.
    • Young children can enjoy coloring a drawing of an agate geode.
    • For the more advanced children of all ages, there will be quizzes to test their knowledge of rocks and minerals.
    • Grab bags generously filled with tumble-polished stones and key chains made with tumble polished stones will be available for one dollar each.  (bags are popular for all ages!)
    • Children are also encouraged to watch the demonstrations being given by club members and ask lots of questions.

  • Door Prizes - get a free door prize ticket when you enter the Show for prizes drawn every half hour
     
  • Prize Drawing - don't miss out on wonderful prizes
     
  • Silent Auction - Our silent auction will have a wide variety of rocks and minerals.
     
  • Snack Bar - great snacks and beverages conveniently available in the next building, with a comfortable seating area.

Enjoy the show in a setting that is rich in history and plentiful in present day interests.
